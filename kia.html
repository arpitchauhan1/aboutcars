Kia Cars

Kia car price starts at Rs 6.72 Lakh for the cheapest model which is Sonet and the price of most expensive model, which is Carnival starts at Rs 24.95 Lakh. Kia offers 3 car models in India, including 3 cars in SUV/MUV category. Kia has 1 upcoming car in India, Rio. Kia Motors Corporation, also known as Kia Motors, is a South Korea based automobile manufacturer. The company has its global headquarters located in Seoul. A minority stake of the brand is owned by Hyundai Motor Corporation. Kia Motors has made its debut in the Indian market with the launch of the Seltos. Kia Motors is set to make its debut in the Indian market with the launch of its first product, the Seltos, on 22 August 2019. The Seltos is a mid-size SUV that is produced at the company’s manufacturing facility located in Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh. The annual production of this plant stands at 3 lakh units. The Kia Seltos will be sold via 260 touch-points located across the country. Apart from the Seltos, Kia Motors has planned a few more product launches across segments. A few of these models are likely to include the Grand Carnival luxury MPV, a new small SUV, and a new mid-size MPV.
